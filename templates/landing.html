How to Play

  • Use Arrow Keys to move through the maze.
  • Stay on the paths — you cannot move through walls.
  • Hitting a wall: To break it, you must answer a question correctly.
  • Plan your route — some walls may block shortcuts.
  • Reach the exit to escape the maze of echoes.
  • Tip: Wrong answers may delay your progress, so think carefully before breaking a wall.
